At SportsMagicBox, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your information is handled responsibly. To provide functionality and enhance app performance, we may collect:

  • Device Information: Non-identifiable information such as device type, operating system, and app version.

The data we collect is used for:

5. Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected, we do not retain user-specific data. Any analytics data is anonymized and stored according to third-party service policies.
