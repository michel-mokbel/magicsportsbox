Privacy Policy for MovieMagicBox

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

At MovieMagicBox, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the data we process, how it is used, and your rights. By using the MovieMagicBox application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Personal Data: MovieMagicBox does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or payment details since no login, sign-up, or transactions are required.
  • No User Accounts: We do not store any user credentials or profile information.

2. Information We May Collect

To provide functionality and enhance app performance, we may collect:

  • Device Information: Non-identifiable information like device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data to understand app usage and improve features. Examples include app crashes, screen views, and session duration.

3. How We Use Collected Data

  • To ensure compatibility and improve app performance.
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and enhance the user experience.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

MovieMagicBox integrates the following third-party services, which may process data per their privacy policies:

  • OMDb Movies API: Used to fetch movie details, including titles, genres, ratings, and posters. This data is used only within the app and is not stored or shared elsewhere. Read their Privacy Policy.

  • Analytics Tools: Aggregated data (e.g., Firebase Analytics) may be used to measure app performance without collecting personal information.

5. Data Retention

Since no personal information is collected, MovieMagicBox does not retain user-specific data. Any analytics data collected is anonymized and stored according to the policies of third-party services.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard practices to secure our app and protect against unauthorized access or misuse. However, as with any online platform, complete security cannot be guaranteed. We recommend users take precautions when using online services.

7. Children's Privacy

MovieMagicBox is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If we become aware that data from a child under 13 has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

8. User Rights

As no personal data is collected, users do not need to request data access, deletion, or corrections. For inquiries about app functionality or this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
